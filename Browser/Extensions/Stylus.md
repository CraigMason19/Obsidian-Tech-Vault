A [[browser]] extension that allows you to add your own [[CSS]] to websites, altering how it is rendered in the browser.

Allows you to render the page the way that you want e.g. 
- Altering or even removing elements.
- Changing colours
- Changing fonts

[My Stylus Scripts](https://github.com/CraigMason19/Scripts/tree/master/Stylus)

---

Stylus injects a custom `<style>` block into the page after a sites own CSS loads. As the custom rules come later they win in the cascade, especially when used with `!important`.
